Things To Do

What the Bay does best

Hand-picked experiences for every type of traveler — outdoor adventurer, foodie, culture seeker, or day-tripper.

🌉
Landmarks
Walk the Golden Gate Bridge

The south end offers the best angle. Go on a clear morning before the fog rolls in — usually before 10am.

Free
🌲
Nature
Hike Marin Headlands

Miles of coastal trails with sweeping bay views, WWII bunkers, and a black-sand beach that's rarely crowded.

Free entry
🍷
Day Trip
Napa & Sonoma Wine Tours

World-class wineries less than 90 minutes from the city. Book tastings in advance, especially weekends.

$25–$85+
🥟
Food
Dim Sum in the Richmond

SF's Richmond District has some of the best dim sum outside Hong Kong. Arrive early or expect a long wait.

Under $20
⛵
Water
Bay Cruise & Alcatraz

The audio tour on Alcatraz is genuinely excellent. Book tickets at least 2 weeks ahead in peak season.

$45/person
🎨
Culture
SFMOMA & Mission Murals

World-class modern art inside, then walk the Mission for street murals that rival any gallery wall.

$25 SFMOMA
🌅
Views
Twin Peaks at Sunset

Best 360° panoramic view of the city. Dress warm — it's always windier and colder up top than you expect.

Free
🚴
Active
Bike to Sausalito

Rent bikes at Fisherman's Wharf, ride across the Golden Gate to Sausalito, then catch the ferry back.

~$35 rental
